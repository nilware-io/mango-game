A surrealist 3d video game based on art, derealization and airplanes



Mango is a game in progress that explores the idea of derealization and peoples' tendency to try and escape from real life through art and dreams. It features unique, artistic level design and evocative exploration, often triggering your feelings and memories to create a full, lived-in experience.

Developed by Pavlos Paliouras-Margaritis, Konstantinos Kamaropoulos and Stefanos Kornilios Mitsis Poiitidis.
